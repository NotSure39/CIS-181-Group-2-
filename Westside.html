Garfield Park Conservatory

A botanical conservatory is one of the most stunning in the nation, and Chicago's Garfield Park Conservatory is one of the biggest. A collection of eight indoor displays gardens displays thousands of plant species from around the world, making the Conservatory often described as "landscape art under glass.". In addition to indoor gardens and tropical temperatures, there are more than 10 acres of outdoor gardens, nature play areas for children, water lily ponds, and other attractions. We hope you enjoy your visit to Garfield Park Conservatory, whether you're relaxing after a busy day in the city or attending one of our upcoming programs or events.
