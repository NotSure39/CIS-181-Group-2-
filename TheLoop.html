The Loop

The Loop is the heart of Chicago, boasting a plethora of activities and iconic buildings. Explore a list of what you can do next time you're in the Loop!

Art Institute

One of the oldest and largest museums in the world with a permanent collection of nearly 300,000 works of art.

Shedd Aquarium

An indoor public aquarium with over 32,000 animals, including fish, marine mammals, birds, snakes, amphibians, and insects.

Field Museum of Natural History

One of the largest museums in the world focusing on educational and scientific programs, with an extensive scientific specimen and artifact collections.

Adler Planetarium

The first planetarium in the United States, Adler Planetarium is dedicated to astronomy and astrophysics, aiming to inspire space exploration.

Grant Park

319 acre public park, home to Buckingham Fountain and several large annual events.

Millennium Park

Public park featuring a variety of public art, outdoor spaces, and venues.

Navy Pier

3,300-foot-long pier encompassing parks, shops, restaurants, and more!

Willis Tower

110 story skyscrapper known for its observation Skydeck.
